AI Detection IB AP Data Study February 2026

AI Writing in Schools: What the Data Tells Us

A comprehensive data study examining AI writing detection across AP Language and IB Diploma assessments in our school. Presents findings from two amnesty studies (91 papers), an internal Turnitin validation study, IB Cohort 14 Extended Essay and TOK Essay data (68 submissions), and a meta-analysis of third-party research on detection precision. Documents a 63% increase in flagging rates between September 2025 and January 2026, and proposes a supervised writing protocol for flagged assessments.

About This Site

This site documents classroom and departmental research conducted at the An International School. Studies are based on real student data, collected ethically and anonymously, and are produced to inform institutional policy and teaching practice. All student information has been anonymised. Reports are intended for an academic and administrative audience.
